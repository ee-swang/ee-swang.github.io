Short Bio

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Whiting School of Engineering, Johns Hopkins University, Baltimore, MD, USA. I received both my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from the College of Electrical Engineering, Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China. I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at Missouri University of Science and Technology (formerly University of Missouri-Rolla), Rolla, MO, USA. My research interests include power system operation and planning, renewable energy integration, and applications of energy storage technology in power systems.
