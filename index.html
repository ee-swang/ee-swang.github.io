Short Bio

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Missouri University of Science and Technology (formerly University of Missouri-Rolla), Rolla, MO, USA. I received both my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from the College of Electrical Engineering, Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China. My research interests include power system operation and planning, renewable energy integration, and applications of energy storage technology in power systems.
